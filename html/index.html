I first got excited about photography when I was gifted my first camera by my dad. It was his old point-and-shoot that he would use to take photos of animals and landscapes during his hiking excursions. Initially, I viewed the camera as a way to connect with my dad who at the time, I had very little in common with. But, soon enough, I began to develop my own passion for photography separate from that of my dads. Nowadays I am drawn to utilizing photography to tell stories and document memories not only for my own personal projects but for those who trust me with their special moments.
